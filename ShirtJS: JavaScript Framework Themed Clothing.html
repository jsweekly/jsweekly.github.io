Frequently Asked Questions

Who is behind this?

We are a small team of designers and developers based in Atlanta. We noticed that developers didn’t have any cool swag to show of the expertise and passion they had for their preferred JavaScript frameworks.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Each order takes 2-5 business days to be fulfilled, then is shipped with USPS (or DirectLink if ordered is outside the US and Canada). US orders take 4-7 business days to be delivered after fulfillment and International orders take 7-21 business days for delivery after fulfillment.

What is ShirtJS' return and exchange policy?

All sales are final. However we try to be accommodating and a shirt may be exchanged if it’s is defective and returned in within 30 days of ship date.

You’re are financially responsible for shipping the item back to us. When returning items, we suggest you insure the package via Mail or FedEx and purchase tracking services for your protection. Save the shipping receipt for your records. Only merchandise received by our warehouse can be processed.

Will my International Order incur a Customs Fee?

Not all International orders will have a customs fee but some may depending on your country of residence. This depends on size of package, country limits, order value and variety of other factors. Customers will be responsible for any customs fee.

How do I pay for my order? Is it secure?

We accept PayPal and Credit Cards securely through Snipcart via 128 bit Encryption. Both of them are regarded as the top payment processing platforms on the internet and both are very secure.

How Do I Care for My T-shirt?

All of ShirtJS t-shirts should be washed inside out, in cold water and tumbled dry on low heat. For more specific instructions, please refer to the washing instructions printed on each shirt’s label.

How is your apparel made?

All of our designs are printed with latest t-shirt printing technology (DTG). We use eco-friendly, water based Inks to print ourshirts. This gives the apparel a softer, more comfortable feel compared to traditional screen print but it is just as durable.

If you notice any off-white residue on your t-shirt when you receive it, don’t worry! This is special solution we use for printing and will come off after the first wash cycle.
